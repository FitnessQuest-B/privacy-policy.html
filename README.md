Privacy Policy for TYR
Last updated: December 2025
TYR respects your privacy. This app is designed to be offline-first and does not sell or share your personal data.
Information We Collect
TYR may store the following information locally on your device:
Workout and exercise logs
Training routines and program data
Body measurements and fitness-related stats (if entered by the user)
TYR does not collect financial information, contacts, messages, or precise location data.
How Your Data Is Used
Your data is used solely to:
Display your workout history
Analyze training progress
Improve your personal fitness tracking experience
Data Sharing
TYR does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
Data Storage
All data is stored locally on your device.
Optional cloud sync features may be added in the future; if so, this policy will be updated accordingly.
Data Deletion
You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app or using in-app reset options where available.
Children’s Privacy
TYR is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
